## Decisions Made

### Decision 1: Q2 2026 OKRs — Fitness, Finance, AMB GTM
- **Context:** LifeOS needs active goals to track from Day 1 — needed to populate goals-log.md with real Q2 objectives
- **Decision:** Three OKRs confirmed by Hassan:

  - G003: AMB GTM Launch (Stripe wired Apr 15, 3 LinkedIn posts May 1, first paying customer Jun 30)
- **Rationale:** These are Hassan's stated top priorities for Q2. All three are measurable with specific key results.
- **Impact:** Goals-log.md seeded with all three OKRs in full OKR format. G003 directly maps to AQ-001 (Stripe) already in the action queue.

### Decision 2: Phase 1 Launches with All 4 Hassan-Selected Domains Simultaneously
- **Context:** Needed to define what Phase 1 looks like operationally
- **Decision:** Health, Finance, Projects-Work, and Goals-Ideas all activate on Day 1 (no staged domain unlock within Phase 1)
- **Rationale:** All 4 logs and agents are ready. Artificial sequencing would reduce value without reducing friction.
- **Impact:** Day 1 protocol defined as a one-time "current state snapshot" across all 4 domains before regular check-ins begin

### Decision 3: 30-Day Habit Formation Plan with Weekly Focuses
- **Context:** Habit formation requires ~21–30 days of consistency — needed a structured ramp to avoid early drop-off
- **Decision:** 4-week progression: Week 1 = show up (no quality pressure), Week 2 = log quality, Week 3 = queue discipline, Week 4 = first briefing
- **Rationale:** Don't try to do everything perfectly on Day 1. Build the reflex first, then raise quality.
- **Impact:** Friction reduction rules defined: brief 1-sentence check-ins acceptable in Phase 1, no guilt-tripping for missed days

### Decision 4: Phase 2 Gate = Day 30 Success Criteria
- **Context:** Needed a quality gate before adding Relationships and Plans domains
- **Decision:** Phase 2 unlocks when: ≥5 check-ins/week for 4 weeks + first briefing generated + Critical queue (AQ-001 to AQ-005) resolved/in-progress + 4 weeks of OKR data logged
- **Rationale:** Expanding scope before the habit is locked creates overwhelm and failure. Phase 2 only adds value if Phase 1 is working.
- **Impact:** Relationships and Plans domains held until Week 5+ (per Hassan's readiness)

### Decision 5: Fully Operational LifeOS = 5 Criteria at Month 3+
- **Context:** Needed to define what "done" looks like so there's a clear success target
- **Decision:** LifeOS is fully operational when: (1) ≥4x/week check-in is a natural habit, (2) briefing used before major decisions, (3) action queue is single source of truth, (4) at least 1 quarterly review completed, (5) Claude pattern observations have influenced at least 1 real decision
- **Rationale:** "Fully operational" means the system is actively changing behavior — not just logging data
- **Impact:** Criterion 5 (pattern influencing decisions) is the north star that distinguishes LifeOS from a journal
