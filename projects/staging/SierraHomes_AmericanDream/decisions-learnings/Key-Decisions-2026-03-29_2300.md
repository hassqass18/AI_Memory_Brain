## Decisions Made

### Decision 1: Building Configuration — Ground + 11 Floors
- **Decision:** 12-storey tower (ground + 11), ~9 units per floor across 108 units
- **Impact:** Structural and MEP engineering scope confirmed at high-rise scale; NCA and Mombasa County permit requirements apply

### Decision 2: Unit Sizes Confirmed
- **Decision:** 1BR = 966 sqft (89.7 sqm), 2BR = 1,311 sqft (121.8 sqm), 3BR = 2,617 sqft (243.1 sqm)
- **Impact:** Total residential GFA = 195,996 sqft / 18,208 sqm. Hard cost rate = KES 4,041/sqft / KES 43,498/sqm. Units are generously sized — strong value proposition vs. Nairobi comps.

### Decision 3: Premium Amenity Stack Including Rooftop Movie Theatre & Bar
- **Decision:** Amenities include 24hr security, parking, pool, gym, and a rooftop movie theatre and bar
- **Rationale:** Rooftop cinema is highly differentiated in Mombasa/Nyali market — no comparable offering at this scale
- **Impact:** Rooftop amenity to be centrepiece of off-plan marketing campaign; adds to soft costs and construction complexity; must be reflected in sales brochure prominently

### Decision 4: Contractor — Tender Not Yet Issued
- **Decision:** Main contractor procurement deferred until after permit approval
- **Impact:** No contractor risk yet; tender process should be initiated in parallel with or immediately after permit submission to avoid delays post-approval

### Decision 5: Financing — Equity Investors for KES ~326M Gap
- **Decision:** The ~KES 326M gap between off-plan revenue (KES 584M) and total development cost (KES 910M) will be funded by equity investors
- **Impact:** Investor deck and term sheet are critical path items; equity terms (share, return, exit) must be defined before construction starts; off-plan sales must be on schedule to minimise equity drawdown

### Decision 6: Cost Structure Confirmed
- **Decision:** Hard costs = KES 792,000,000 (87% of total); Soft costs = KES 117,961,921 (13% of total)
- **Impact:** Soft cost breakdown to be itemised (architecture, QS, legal, permits, marketing, contingency)

### Decision 7: Post-Delivery Management — TBD
- **Decision:** Management model not yet decided
- **Recommendation:** Professional property management firm — buyers will ask before purchase
- **Action:** Define management model before off-plan sales launch; set and disclose service charge to buyers
