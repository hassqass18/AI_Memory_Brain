## What Claude Will Do on Day 1

Day 1 is a one-time orientation — slightly different from a regular check-in. It has two parts:

### Part 1 — Current State Snapshot (~15 minutes)

Claude will ask you a short baseline assessment across all 4 Phase 1 domains. Answer honestly — this sets your starting point for all Q2 OKR tracking.

**Health (G001 baseline):**
- How many hours did you sleep last night?
- When did you last work out, and what did you do?
- Rate your current morning energy: 1–5
- Rate your current workout consistency: how many days/week on average lately?

**Finance (G002 baseline):**
- Rough sense of current cash position — comfortable, tight, or unclear?
- Any income coming in this week or month you're expecting?
- Any large expenses or payments due soon?

**Projects & Work:**
- What's the #1 thing you need to move on each active project this week?
  - SierraHomes: ____
  - MSIFS: ____
  - ProvectaGroup: ____
  - AMB: ____
  - PersonalBranding: ____

**Goals & Ideas:**
- Confirms Q2 OKRs (G001, G002, G003) — any changes?
- Any ideas you've been sitting on that should be captured today?

### Part 2 — Action Queue Review (~10 minutes)

Claude will walk through the 🔴 Critical items (AQ-001, AQ-004, AQ-021, AQ-024) and confirm:
- Which are you doing today?
- Which are you doing this week?
- Anything re-prioritized?

Then Claude writes the Day 1 baseline entries into all domain logs and delivers your first LifeOS briefing.

## What Happens After Day 1

Regular check-ins take ~10 minutes. Free narration — Claude listens, extracts, logs, updates the queue, and asks 2–4 follow-up questions if needed. That's it.

Week 1 goal: show up. Quality comes with repetition.
