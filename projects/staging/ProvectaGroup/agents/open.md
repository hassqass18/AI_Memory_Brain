# open.md — Staging Session Initialization Protocol
## ProvectaGroup

**Purpose:** Execute at the start of any session working on Provecta Group operations or client engagements.

---

> **Prompt for AI:** "Execute the ProvectaGroup staging session initialization:
>
> 1. **Read Master Context:** Read `Master-Context.md` — understand Provecta Group's current engagements, operational standards, and priorities.
>
> 2. **Check Prior Decisions:** Read `Key-Decisions.md` — scan for keywords matching your current task.
>
> 3. **Check Session History:** Read `Sessions.md` — scan for prior sessions relevant to your current task.
>
> 4. **Check Progress:** Read `NextSteps.md` — understand what's been completed and what's pending.
>
> 5. **Load Relevant Agents:** Based on what you're working on today, read the relevant advisor files:
>    - Client contracts / legal → `Legal/agents/Legal-advisor.md`
>    - Operations / process design → `Operations/agents/Operations-advisor.md`
>    - Automation / workflows → `Operations/agents/Automation-advisor.md`
>    - Marketing / business development → `Marketing/agents/Marketing-advisor.md`
>    - Revenue / pricing strategy → `Finance/agents/Finance-advisor.md`
>    - Strategic planning → `Executive/agents/Strategy-advisor.md`
>
> 6. **Create Decision Log:** Create `decisions-learnings/Key-Decisions-[YYYY-MM-DD]_[HHMM].md` and update incrementally throughout the session.
>
> 7. **Begin Work:** Ask the user what they want to focus on today, or continue from NextSteps.md."
