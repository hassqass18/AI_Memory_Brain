## Phase Objective

Establish the legal, financial, marketing, and sales foundation required to launch the SierraHomes_AmericanDream off-plan campaign. Achieve 20+ buyer reservations before permit receipt, validating market demand and building the pipeline for formal SPA conversion in Phase 2. Simultaneously secure the ~KES 326M equity investment gap needed to fund construction.
