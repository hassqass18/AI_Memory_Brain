## Decisions Made

### Decision 1: Sales Model — For-Sale Only, 40/60 Split
- **Decision:** All 106/108 units to be sold (no rental). 40% off-plan (~43 units) during construction; 60% on-plan (~65 units) at/near completion.
- **Rationale:** Off-plan sales recoup the bulk (~64%) of the KES 910M development cost during construction; on-plan sales deliver the project profit margin.
- **Impact:** Marketing and sales strategy must prioritise off-plan pipeline immediately. Financing gap of ~KES 326M requires construction loan or equity bridge.

### Decision 2: Unit Mix — 24×1BR, 36×2BR, 48×3BR
- **Decision:** Final unit mix confirmed at 24 one-bedroom, 36 two-bedroom, 48 three-bedroom units.
- **Impact:** ⚠️ Total = 108 units vs. stated 106. 2-unit discrepancy must be resolved before permits are filed.

### Decision 3: Pricing Structure — Off-Plan / On-Plan Differential
- **Decision:** Pricing set at a 28–50% premium between off-plan and on-plan:
  - 1BR: KES 6M off-plan / KES 9M on-plan (+50%)
  - 2BR: KES 13M off-plan / KES 17M on-plan (+31%)
  - 3BR: KES 18M off-plan / KES 23M on-plan (+28%)
- **Rationale:** Off-plan discount incentivises early buyers and funds construction; on-plan premium captures full market value at delivery.

### Decision 4: Target Buyer Segments
- **Decision:** Primary targets are Kenyan diaspora and local property investors; secondary target is international investors.
- **Rationale:** Nyali is a premium location attractive to diaspora and investors; diaspora buyers are proven off-plan purchasers in the Kenyan market.
- **Impact:** Marketing strategy must include diaspora channels (UK, US, Gulf), international listing portals, and Nairobi investor networks.

### Decision 5: Delivery Date — August 2029
- **Decision:** Target delivery and handover set for August 2029.
- **Impact:** Construction programme must be planned backwards from August 2029. Permit timeline is critical path.

### Decision 6: Development Cost — KES 909,961,921
- **Decision:** Total development cost is KES 909,961,921 (two components: KES 117,961,921 + KES 792,000,000).
- **⚠️ Open:** Nature of the two cost components (land vs. construction, hard vs. soft costs) not yet clarified. Required for Document 2 financial model.
