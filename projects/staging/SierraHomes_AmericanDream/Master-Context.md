## Document 1: Project Context [COMPLETE]

### Vision & Goals

- **Vision Statement:** SierraHomes_AmericanDream is a 108-unit residential apartment complex located in Nyali, Mombasa, Kenya. It delivers modern, high-quality housing to one of East Africa's most desirable coastal urban markets, targeting diaspora buyers, local property investors, and international investors seeking premium Mombasa real estate.
- **Primary Goal:** Achieve 40% off-plan unit sales (~43 units) during construction to recoup the bulk of the development budget, then sell the remaining 60% (~65 units) at or near completion to realise project profit.
- **Sales Model:** For-sale only (no rental). Off-plan sales are the primary construction financing mechanism alongside the development budget.
- **Secondary Goals:**
  - Establish SierraHomes / Provecta Group as a credible residential developer in the Mombasa market
  - Deliver on time and on budget by August 2029
  - Achieve full sell-out within 12 months of delivery
  - Build a buyer pipeline and brand presence for future phases or projects

---

### Target Audience

| Segment | Description | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Kenyan diaspora | Kenyans living abroad (UK, US, Gulf, etc.) investing in Mombasa property | Primary |
| Local property investors | Mombasa/Nairobi-based investors buying to resell or hold | Primary |
| International investors | Non-Kenyan foreign buyers seeking East Africa real estate exposure | Secondary |
| Owner-occupiers | Mombasa residents upgrading or buying a first home | Opportunistic |

**Buyer motivation:** Investment returns, capital appreciation in Nyali, lifestyle/retirement asset, diaspora connection to Mombasa.
