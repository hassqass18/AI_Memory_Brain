## Decisions Made

### Decision 1: Pre-Launch Sales Immediately (Before Permit)
- **Decision:** Begin taking reservations and EOIs now, before permit is received. Convert to binding SPAs on permit approval (Q3 2026).
- **Rationale:** Builds pipeline early; no legal risk as reservations are non-binding; maximises time to hit 43-unit off-plan target before construction cost peaks
- **Impact:** Marketing must start immediately. Reservation agreement to be drafted by legal counsel. Target 20+ reservations before permit.

### Decision 2: Permit Timeline — Q3 2026
- **Decision:** Mombasa County construction permit expected within 3 months (Q3 2026)
- **Impact:** Construction start is Q3 2026; this makes August 2029 delivery achievable with 36-month build. Critical path: any permit delay >3 months risks the delivery date.

### Decision 3: 4-Phase Project Lifecycle
- **Phase 1** (Now – Q2 2026): Pre-launch — reservations, equity raise, brochure, site prep
- **Phase 2** (Q3 2026 – Q2 2027): Off-plan launch — permit, contracts, groundbreaking, 43-unit target
- **Phase 3** (Q3 2027 – Aug 2029): Construction & on-plan sales
- **Phase 4** (Aug 2029 – Aug 2030): Handover & sell-out

### Decision 4: Off-Plan Sales Priority — 3BR First
- **Decision:** Prioritise 3BR and 2BR unit sales in the off-plan phase; 1BR units last
- **Rationale:** 1BR blended revenue (KES 7.75M) is below average dev cost per unit (KES 8.43M); 3BR and 2BR carry the project margin
- **Impact:** Sales scripts, agent briefings, and campaign targeting must lead with 3BR and 2BR

### Decision 5: Revenue Phasing Agreed
- Pre-launch deposits (now–Q2 2026): ~KES 120M
- Off-plan contracts (Q3 2026–Q2 2027): KES 584M
- On-plan during construction (Q1 2028–Aug 2029): ~KES 899M
- Final sell-out (Aug 2029–Aug 2030): ~KES 148M
