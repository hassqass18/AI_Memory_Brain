**Rule:** Ideas are NEVER deleted. They are promoted, parked, or dropped — but always logged.

---

## How Ideas Are Captured

During daily check-in, if you mention an idea, Claude logs it here immediately. Format:

```
## [YYYY-MM-DD] — [Idea Title]
**Domain:** [Health / Finance / Business / Personal / Other]
**Idea:** [describe it fully — don't be brief, you won't remember later]
**Why it matters:** [one sentence on the opportunity or value]
**Stage:** Raw Idea / Being Explored / Parked / Promoted to Plan
**Next action:** [or "None yet"]
```

---

## Stage Definitions

- **Raw Idea:** Just captured — no evaluation yet
- **Being Explored:** Actively thinking about or researching
- **Parked:** Good idea, wrong time — revisit later
- **Promoted to Plan:** Moved to `Plans/plans-log.md` with deliverables and timeline
- **Dropped:** Not pursuing — logged with reason
