# Freepik Real Estate Flyer Template — Design Analysis
> Source: [Business Flyer Design — Freepik](https://www.freepik.com/free-vector/business-flyer-design_3778812.htm)
> Author: m.salama
> Use: Reference template for Sierra Homes marketing flyer adaptation

---

## Overview

A clean, professional A4 portrait real estate sales flyer. The layout is bold and direct — designed to communicate a property listing at a glance. Uses a strong two-color scheme (red + dark charcoal) over a white base with two photo slots and a clear contact footer.

---

## Layout Structure (Top to Bottom)

The flyer is divided into **5 stacked horizontal sections**, each with a distinct purpose and visual weight.

---

### SECTION 1 — Hero Image (Full-Width Photo)
**Height proportion:** ~35% of total flyer

- Full-width property photograph spanning the entire top of the flyer
- No padding or border — image bleeds to the edges on left, right, and top
- **Logo block** overlaid in the top-left corner of the image:
  - White background box with slight padding
  - Circular icon/emblem in red (location pin / diamond motif)
  - "YOUR LOGO" in bold dark text
  - "YOUR TAGLINE HERE" in small red italic text beneath
- The logo sits inside the image rather than above it, keeping the top margin clean

**Design note:** The hero image should be a wide landscape exterior shot. Interior or lifestyle images do not work as well here — the property's curb appeal is the visual hook.

---

### SECTION 2 — Title Banner (Full-Width Red Bar)
**Height proportion:** ~10% of total flyer

A bold full-width red (`#E61C28`) horizontal bar immediately below the hero image, no gap.

**Left side (60% width):**
- Line 1: `PROPERTY` — large, bold, white, uppercase sans-serif
- Line 2: `FOR SALE` — same size, bold, white, uppercase
- A thin white vertical divider line separates the left text block from the right price block

**Right side (40% width):**
- Small label: `OFFERED AT` — tiny, white, uppercase, regular weight (sits above the price)
- Price: `$3,00,000` — large, bold, white

**Design note:** The red bar creates an immediate strong contrast against both the photo above and the white content area below. The price is immediately visible without the reader needing to search for it.

---

### SECTION 3 — Primary Content Block (White Background)
**Height proportion:** ~15% of total flyer

White background. Two-column layout within this block.

**Left column (~70% width):**
- `HEADLINE` — large, bold, red, left-aligned (acts as the property's selling tagline or development name)
- Body paragraph — small, dark gray, regular weight, 4–5 lines of descriptive copy
  - Example: Property highlights, key selling points, development overview

**Right column (~30% width):**
- QR code image — right-aligned, square, moderate size
- Small caption below QR: `Lorem Ipsum Scan Here` — tiny, gray, centered under the code
- The QR links to the project website, brochure, or booking form

**Spacing:** ~16px internal padding on all sides. The headline and body are left-aligned, the QR is right-aligned within its column.

---

### SECTION 4 — Feature Block (Text + Secondary Image)
**Height proportion:** ~25% of total flyer

White background continues. Two-column layout.

**Left column (~58% width):**
- Sub-headline (two-tone styling):
  - `WE DESIGN` — bold, red, uppercase
  - `MODERN ELEGANT INTERIOR & EXTERIOR` — bold, dark charcoal, uppercase (continuation on same and next line)
- Body paragraph — small, dark gray, regular weight, 6–8 lines
  - Example: Amenity highlights, construction quality, lifestyle pitch

**Right column (~42% width):**
- Secondary photo — rectangular (portrait orientation), right-aligned
- Suggested subject: Interior shot, lifestyle image, or key handover / agent photo
- Image fills the column width and aligns to the right edge of the content area

**Design note:** The two-tone sub-headline (red first word + charcoal remainder) is a recurring typographic device throughout this template — it draws the eye to the action word while keeping the full phrase readable.

---

### SECTION 5 — Contact Footer (Dark Bar)
**Height proportion:** ~15% of total flyer

Full-width dark charcoal bar (`#1E1E1E` / near-black). No top border, abrupt transition from white content above.

**Internal layout — two columns with a red vertical accent divider:**

**Left column (~55% width):**
- Header: `Get In Touch With:` — small, white, bold
- Contact details list (small, white, regular weight):
  - `Phone:` +441-273-9909
  - `Fax:` 256.460.815
  - `Website:` www.example.com
  - `Email:` info@example.com
  - `Address:` 701 Raymond Rocks Road, Walla Walla, OR 99362
- A thin red vertical accent line separates left from right columns

**Right column (~45% width):**
- Label: `Call` — medium, white, regular weight, centered
- Phone number: `+69 6738 9412` — very large, bold, white, centered
- The large phone number is the dominant visual element in this column — designed to be readable at a glance

## Visual / Decorative Elements

- **Thin vertical red line** — used as a divider in the title banner (separating name from price) and in the footer (separating contact details from phone number). Consistent decorative motif.
- **Logo overlay box** — white background pill/rectangle over the hero image, top-left. Keeps the brand visible without competing with the photo.
- **QR code** — positioned in the top-right of the primary content block. Provides a digital bridge from print to web.
- **Two-photo layout** — one full-width hero + one smaller secondary portrait image. The secondary image is right-aligned to create text wrap on the left.
- **No borders or drop shadows** — the template relies entirely on background color changes (white → red → white → dark) to define sections. Clean, flat aesthetic.
