## Decisions Made

### Decision 1: Check-In Style — Free Narration + Claude Follow-Ups
- **Context:** Needed to decide how Hassan feeds data into LifeOS daily
- **Options:** Free narration only; Claude-prompted questions; both; domain-by-domain walkthrough
- **Decision:** Both — free narration + targeted Claude follow-ups (2–4 questions max)
- **Rationale:** Free narration is zero-friction and captures nuance. Claude follow-ups fill structured gaps without forcing Hassan to think in templates.
- **Impact:** Check-in protocol designed with 6-step workflow: narrate → parse → follow-ups → log → queue update → confirmation

### Decision 2: Action Queue Prioritized by Urgency × Impact
- **Context:** Needed a consistent prioritization method for the live action queue
- **Options:** By domain; by due date; by energy level; Urgency × Impact matrix
- **Decision:** Urgency × Impact matrix (PM-standard)
- **Rationale:** Surfaces what's most time-sensitive AND consequential regardless of domain. Prevents low-impact urgent items from displacing high-impact medium-urgency items.
- **Impact:** 4-tier priority system: 🔴 Critical (9), 🟠 Important (6), 🟡 Do Later (3–4), ⚪ Backlog (1–2). Queue file created at `Executive/action-queue.md` with 20 pre-seeded items from existing NextSteps.md.

### Decision 3: Briefing Cadence — On Demand
- **Context:** Needed to decide whether briefings are scheduled or generated on request
- **Options:** Sunday evening; Monday morning; Friday afternoon; on demand
- **Decision:** On demand — Hassan generates when he needs it
- **Rationale:** Forcing a fixed schedule creates pressure and can become a chore. Hassan generates the briefing when it's genuinely useful.
- **Impact:** Briefing trigger phrase: "Generate briefing." Briefings saved to `Executive/briefings/briefing-YYYY-MM-DD.md`.

### Decision 4: All 4 Phase 1 Domains Activate Simultaneously
- **Context:** Needed to choose which domains go live first
- **Options:** Subset (1–2 domains); all 4 Hassan selected; all 6 immediately
- **Decision:** All 4 Hassan selected (Health, Finance, Projects-Work, Goals-Ideas) activate on Day 1. Relationships + Plans added in Phase 2 (Week 5+).
- **Rationale:** Hassan selected all 4 — no reason to artificially limit. These 4 cover the highest-value domains for his current situation.
- **Impact:** Phase 1 log files created for all 4 domains. Phase 2 files (Relationships, Plans) already exist and ready.

### Decision 5: Log Format — Dated Block Entries, Append-Only
- **Context:** Needed to define how data is stored in domain logs
- **Decision:** Each domain has one running `.md` log file. Claude appends dated block entries. Never overwrites or deletes.
- **Rationale:** Preserves full history. Enables pattern analysis in Phase 3 Intelligence Layer. Simple, portable, no database required.
- **Impact:** 6 domain log files created (+ 1 ideas backlog). All use consistent dated-block format.

### Decision 6: Action Queue Seeded from Existing NextSteps.md
- **Context:** Hassan has 20+ pending action items already tracked at the root level
- **Decision:** Pre-seed the LifeOS action queue with existing pending items from root `NextSteps.md` and `agents/open.md` workspace index
- **Rationale:** Don't make Hassan re-enter what's already tracked. LifeOS should ingest the current reality on Day 1.
- **Impact:** `Executive/action-queue.md` created with AQ-001 through AQ-020 pre-seeded across all 4 priority tiers.
