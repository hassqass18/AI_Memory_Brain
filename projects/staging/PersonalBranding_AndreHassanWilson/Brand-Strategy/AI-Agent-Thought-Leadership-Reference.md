## 2. LinkedIn Algorithm Intelligence (2025-2026)

### Core Algorithm Drivers (Ranked)

1. **Dwell Time (MOST CRITICAL)** — Time users spend reading your content and comments. Long-form posts with extended reading time get exponential distribution.
2. **Early Engagement (First 60-90 min)** — LinkedIn tests with 2-5% of your network first. Reply to all comments within 60-90 min for 30% engagement boost.
3. **Comment Quality > Quantity** — Substantive 10+ word comments weighted significantly higher. Engagement pods penalized with 97% detection accuracy.
4. **Content Format** — Native formats prioritized. Document/carousel posts get 3-6x higher engagement than text-only.
5. **Author Authority Score** — Consistent expertise in one area rewards broader reach.

### Optimal Post Specifications

| Spec | Recommendation |
|------|---------------|
| **Text length** | 1,000-1,500 characters (triggers extended dwell time) |
| **Best posting times** | Wednesday 4 PM, Friday 3-4 PM, weekday afternoons 3-8 PM |
| **Posting frequency** | 2-5 posts/week (quality over quantity) |
| **Hashtags** | 3-5 per post (mix 1-2 broad + 2-3 niche) |
| **Engagement window** | Reply to ALL comments within first 60-90 minutes |
| **Daily commenting** | 30+ minutes on other creators' posts (10+ word comments) |

**2. List Promise:** "I spent [time] analyzing [topic]. Here's what I found."
**3. Curiosity Gap:** "This [outcome] shouldn't have been possible in [timeframe]. But [reason] changed everything."
**4. Before/After:** "I used to [negative]. Then I discovered [shift]. Now [positive result]."
**5. Provocative Question:** "What if everything you believed about [topic] was wrong?"
