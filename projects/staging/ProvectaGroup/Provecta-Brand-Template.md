# Provecta Group — Brand Identity & Website Template Guide

---

## Brand Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|------|------|----------|-------|
| **Primary** | Provecta Blue | `#1E65B0` | Logo, primary CTAs, links, icons |
| **Dark** | Deep Navy | `#0D2147` | Hero backgrounds, footers, headings |
| **Light** | Ivory Cream | `#F8F4EC` | Section backgrounds, card fills, light panels |
| **Accent** | Warm Gold | `#C8A96E` | Highlight accents, badges, hover states, dividers |
| **Neutral** | White | `#FFFFFF` | Body backgrounds, text on dark |
| **Text** | Charcoal | `#1A1A2E` | Body copy, subheadings |

### Color Rationale
- **Provecta Blue** — Extracted directly from the brand logo. Conveys trust, expertise, and innovation.
- **Deep Navy** — A darker derivation of the primary blue. Adds depth and authority; ideal for executive-level communication.
- **Ivory Cream** — A warm, sophisticated off-white that softens the palette and avoids clinical starkness. Pairs elegantly with navy and gold.
- **Warm Gold** *(suggested)* — A professional accent that bridges navy and cream. Gold signals value, achievement, and premium quality — well-suited for a consulting brand. Avoids the cliché of red or orange.

## Typography

- **Primary Font:** IBM Plex Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Headings:** IBM Plex Sans SemiBold / Bold
- **Body:** IBM Plex Sans Regular, 16px
- **Accent / Labels:** IBM Plex Sans Medium, uppercase tracking

### Type Scale
| Tag | Size | Weight | Use |
|-----|------|--------|-----|
| H1 | 52–60px | Bold | Hero headlines |
| H2 | 40–48px | SemiBold | Section headlines |
| H3 | 28–32px | SemiBold | Card/feature titles |
| H4 | 22–24px | Medium | Sub-section labels |
| Body | 16px | Regular | Paragraphs |
| Small | 13–14px | Regular | Captions, meta |

## Website Template Structure

Based on: [Bisco WP – WowTheme7](https://biscoxwp.wowtheme7.com/?storefront=envato-elements)

---

### 1. Header / Navigation

**Layout:** Fixed top bar, full-width.

**Left:** Logo (image + wordmark)
**Center:** Primary navigation links
**Right:** "Free Consultation" CTA button (filled, accent color)

**Navigation Items:**
- Home
- About Us
- Services (dropdown)
- Projects (dropdown)
- Team
- Blog (dropdown)
- Contact Us
- **[ Free Consultation ]** — CTA Button

---

### 2. Hero Section (Slider / Carousel)

**Type:** Full-width hero with rotating slides (2–3 slides recommended)

**Layout per slide:**
- Background: Dark navy overlay on high-quality image
- **Tag line** (small, gold, uppercase): e.g. "TRUSTED BUSINESS ADVISORS"
- **H1 Headline** (large, white): e.g. "Strategic Consulting That Drives Real Results"
- **Body text** (white, ~2 lines): Brief value proposition
- **Two CTAs:** `[ Explore Services ]` (filled) + `[ Learn More ]` (outlined)

---

### 3. Intro / Welcome Section

**Layout:** Icon + headline + subheadline + two columns of body text + dual CTA buttons

**Content:**
- Section label (small, gold, uppercase)
- H2 headline introducing the firm
- 2-column descriptive paragraph about the company's approach
- Buttons: `[ Our Approach ]` + `[ View Portfolio ]`

---

### 4. Service Cards Section

**Layout:** 4-column grid

**Section Header:**
- Small label (gold, uppercase)
- H2 headline: "What We Do Best"
- Brief intro paragraph

**Each Card Contains:**
- Icon (svg/image, brand blue)
- Card number badge (gold)
- H4 title
- Short description (2–3 sentences)
- `Read More →` link

**Services (suggested for Provecta):**
1. Strategy & Planning
2. Financial Consulting
3. Business Development
4. Operational Excellence

---

### 5. Statistics / Social Proof Banner

**Layout:** Full-width dark background (Deep Navy), 4 stat columns

**Stats:**
- `500+` — Clients Served
- `18` — Years of Experience
- `95%` — Client Retention Rate
- `40+` — Industry Awards

**Optional:** Embedded video play button with background image overlay

---

### 6. Why Choose Us (Tabbed Section)

**Layout:** Left: content tabs | Right: image/video

**Tabs:**
1. Our Philosophy
2. Our Approach
3. Our Vision

**Each tab contains:**
- H3 headline
- Bullet list of 4 key differentiators with icons
- Supporting description

---

### 7. Portfolio / Projects Section

**Layout:** 5-item carousel with image cards

**Each card contains:**
- Featured image
- Category tag
- H4 project title
- Short summary
- `View Project →` link

---

### 8. Team Members Section

**Layout:** 4-column grid

**Section Header:** Label + H2 headline

**Each team card:**
- Profile photo (square or circle crop)
- Full name (linked)
- Title / Role
- Social media icons (LinkedIn, Twitter)

---

### 9. Working Process Section

**Layout:** 4-step horizontal flow with connecting arrows

**Steps:**
1. Initial Consultation
2. Tailored Strategy
3. Implementation
4. Review & Optimize

**Each step:**
- Step number (gold)
- Icon
- H4 title
- 2-sentence description

---

### 10. Testimonials Section

**Layout:** Carousel with centered single quote

**Each testimonial:**
- Client photo (circle)
- Quote (italic, large)
- Client name (bold)
- Title & Company

---

### 11. Blog / Insights Section

**Layout:** 3-column grid (5 posts shown, 3+2 arrangement)

**Each post card:**
- Featured image
- Date + Category tag
- H4 post title (linked)
- Excerpt (2 lines)
- `Read More →` link

---

### 12. Footer

**Layout:** 4-column footer on dark navy background

**Column 1 — Brand:**
- Logo
- Tagline paragraph
- Social media icons

**Column 2 — Quick Links:**
- Services
- About
- Portfolio
- Blog
- Contact

**Column 3 — Recent Posts:**
- 2 post thumbnails with date and title

**Column 4 — Newsletter:**
- H5 headline: "Stay Informed"
- Email input + Subscribe button

**Bottom Bar:**
- Copyright line
- Footer nav: Terms | FAQ | Privacy | Contact

## Responsive Breakpoints

| Breakpoint | Layout |
|---|---|
| Desktop (1280px+) | Full multi-column layouts |
| Tablet (768–1279px) | 2-column grids, collapsed nav |
| Mobile (<768px) | Single column, hamburger menu |

---

## Component Notes

- **Padding standard:** 100–120px top/bottom for all major sections
- **Card border-radius:** 8–12px
- **Button style:** Rounded corners (6px), uppercase label, 14px font
- **CTA Primary:** Deep Navy background, Ivory Cream text
- **CTA Secondary:** Gold border, Gold text, transparent background
- **Icon style:** Outline/line icons, brand blue color
